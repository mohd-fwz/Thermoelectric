The project focuses on energy harvesting using Thermoelectric Generators (TEGs) combined with Phase Change Materials (PCMs). TEGs convert heat directly into electricity when a temperature difference exists across their surfaces. PCMs are materials that absorb or release heat while changing phase (solid ‚Üî liquid) at specific temperatures, allowing them to maintain a constant temperature over time.

Applications
  • Waste Heat Recovery - Harvests the waste heat from electronic components to power small fans or sensors.
  • Automotive energy Saveging - Generates supplementary power for electronics or sensors by converting waste heat using PCMs and TEGs.
  • Self-Powered IoT Devices - Eliminates the need for battery replacement by harvesting ambient heat
